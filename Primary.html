Primary Weapons

Primary Weapons are a player's main means of defense or otherwise known as
the weapon they mainly use. The primary weapon is usually the weapon with
most effectiveness against the infected. Primary weapons can be rifles,
SMGs, LMGs, Flamethrowers, etc.
