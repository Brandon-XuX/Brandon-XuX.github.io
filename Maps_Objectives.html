Maps & Objectives

Maps are the environment and setting where the players play in. Each map
has its own unique features, theme, style, and layout. Maps can be
categorized into normal (includes regular, hardcore, and classic game modes)
and endless which includes an infinite amount of waves while regular and classic
is only limited to 10 and hardcore being 20 waves.

Objectives are similiar to quests that are availale to the players in waves.
Objectives can offer hefty rewards in exp and credits once completed. Only 2
objectives can appear in a map at once.

Objectives Classification

  1. Fill - Requires players to gather items around a map and bring to a desginated vehicle.
  2. Secure - Requires players to hold a position surrounded by a bright circle until the
    percentage of said objective reaches 100%.
  3. Miscellaneous - Does not fall in fill nor secure.
