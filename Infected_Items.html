The Infected & Items

Infected

The infected are the enemies the players face in each wave. The infected are former humansc infected by an unknown pathogen.
There are different types of infected with various health, damage, and abilities. Players are rewarded with credits and exp
when they kill an infected, the reward varies on the type of infected killed. Basic infected are only limited to clawing and
other enhancements. Special infected are stronger and have superior abilities due to mutation from other diseases.
