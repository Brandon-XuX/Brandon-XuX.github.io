Secondary Weapons

Secondary weapons are mainly weapons that supplement and support a primary weapon.
Secondary weapons often are lacking in dps (damage per second), fire rate, range,
accuracy, and more. Secondaries are usually used when a player cannot use their primary
weapon at the situation they are facing, hence a secondary is used. A secondary weapon
usually can be a pistol, PDW (Personal Defense Weapon), Compact SMG, and more.
