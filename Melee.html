Melee Weapons

Melee weapons are hand-held weapons used by players in close quarters combat
against the infected that is within physical range. Such weapons are rarely
if not at all used by players unless under the pretense of having fun due the
extreme poor reliability and risk of using melee weapons against hordes of
the infected.
