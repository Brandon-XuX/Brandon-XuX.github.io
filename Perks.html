Perks

Perks are in a sense similiar to power ups or special skills. A player can select up to 3 perks.
(4 perks of the player has the extra perks gamepass) Perks can increase a player's change of
survivability of increasing a select skill's efficiency. 3 different perks are unlocked at a
certain level (the first unlock being at level 10). 21 perks in total, 7 in each category.

Categories

1. Survivalist Increase physical performance and skills.
2. Craftsman Increases building speed, fortification stats,
and effectiveness.
3. Marksman Increases weapons performance in damage, accuracy, etc.
