About

Welcome to the Those Who Remain fandom wiki.

Those Who Remain is a wave-based zombie shooter game based off of the roblox platform
created by Peak Development Studios officially released in December 21st, 2018.
Players are deployed in 5 minute long waves. The difficulty, number of waves,
and number of infected spawned every round depends on the game mode. As the waves
progressively get higher, the infected have more health, have faster speed, damage, and numbers.
The game ranges from havig a credits system for weapon and skin purchases as well as in-game
loot as well as unlockable perks that function as power ups to increase a player's abilities. The waves
will reset and the maps will change either all the player's have died in a wave or have survived
the final wave.
